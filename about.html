About

Built by operators, for operators.

ai4smartmfg helps small and mid-sized manufacturers adopt AI on the shop floor, in the front office, and in the back office — safely, practically, and with a measurable return.

The premise is simple. The most useful AI for a manufacturer is one that knows the manufacturer’s own work — its SOPs, its drawings, its routings, its people. We bring frontier reasoning to that work, and we keep your data where it must live.
